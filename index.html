Genre Analysis

Sept 28, 2018

This was the first time I wrote a paper on Genre of creating a paper. I learned that it is important to know your audience when it comes to writing a paper.

Read More

For my first reflection in my Rhetoric and Composition II course is the Genre Analysis paper. From the beginning of the year, our professor introduced the class to structuring, genres and the four types of writing. This paper was to help teach the students on how to deicide the type and genre to construct a well written research paper. My Genre Analysis topic paper was on Non-renewable vs. renewable resources and it was to compare two different types of genres revolving around my topic. I picked a research paper vs. a movie. Through comparing these two genres, I have learned that it very important to pick the right genre depending on the audience you want to reach out to and the message behind your paper. With my topic being heavily related to science I found out that it is best to have the information in a typography genre. The reason for this conclusion is based on how much information is needed to make a paper in a research paper. Looking back at the movie I reviewed it I miss out on a lot of information and it obviously focused on the entertainment factor verses the logic behind my genre analysis topic. There is a lot information can be place in a typography genre that is best to inform people which is needed in my topic. This assignment definitely helped me out in this course especially since my topic here is related to my Research Report for this semester which I cover in my next reflection.

Literature Review Research Report

Nov 16, 2018

This research paper has to maybe the 3rd longest I have written, But through it all it changed my perspective on the topic I picked.

Read More

This reflection is on my Literature Review Research Report assignment for the same course, Rhetoric and Composition II. The professor gave us about 1.5 months to research a topic that effects peoples’ everyday life style. The research question or topic I picked was, which renewable resource is the most efficient for the world to invest in? A reason I picked this topic is because it is related to my major and I hope from what I learned from all this well help me educate myself to help my career in chemistry down the road. Also, It’s very exciting for me to help improve humankind in any way. An obstacle I came across this when making this paper was defining the aspect of efficiency. Efficient was hard to define in my paper because there was a lot of variables and it came to pick the ones that made the most sense for my topic. Since, my goal was to inform anybody who is interested in pushing the world to switch to renewable energy, I concluded the efficiency was based on time and money to make the change. This paper has opened my eyes to the idea that detail is key when it comes to writing. I have always been under the impression that science is easy because it’s straight forward however, it involves a lot of details. This paper also enlightened me that solar would probably be the best resources to make a fastest push to making a renewable energy the primary source.
